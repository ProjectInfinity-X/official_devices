Device: Xiaomi Pad 6 (pipa)

Recommended firmware: Latest Firmware Already Included in rom.zip

Flashing Instructions:

* Clean Flash:
- Format Data
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (if you flashed vanilla and want google apps)
- Reboot

* Dirty Flash:
- Wipe Dalvik, Cache
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (Dont forget to reflash the Gapps you used else Gapps will crash )
- Reboot
* OTA:
1. Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)
