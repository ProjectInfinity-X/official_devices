Device: POCO F3 / Redmi K40 / Mi 11X (alioth)
Recommended Recovery: https://orangefox.download/device/alioth
Recommended firmware: 1.0.2.0 (avoid 1.0.3.0, it might brick your phone)

Flashing Instructions:

* Clean Flash:
- Format Data
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (if you flashed vanilla and want google apps)
- Reboot

* Dirty Flash:
- Wipe Dalvik, Cache
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (Dont forget to reflash the Gapps you used else Gapps will crash )
- Reboot

* OTA:
1. Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)
