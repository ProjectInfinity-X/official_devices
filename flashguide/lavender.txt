Device: Redmi Note 7/7s (lavender)
Recommended Recovery: https://sourceforge.net/projects/lc-dev/files/lavender/TWRP-recovery-erofs-dynamic-partitions-230713.img/download

Flashing Instructions:

* Clean Flash:
1. Flash recommended recovery
2. Wipe system, vendor, cache & metadata partition
3. Untoggle "Unmount System before installing a ZIP".
4. Flash the ROM (Ignore system mount error)
5. If Vanilla (Reboot to recovery and then flash Gapps)
6. Format data
7. Reboot and Enjoy

* Dirty Flash:
1. Enter recovery
2. Flash the update
3. Wipe dalvik, cache
4. Reboot and Enjoy
