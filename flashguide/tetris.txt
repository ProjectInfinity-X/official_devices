Device: Nothing CMF Phone 1 (tetris)
Recommended Recovery: https://sourceforge.net/projects/infinity-x/files/tetris/16/recovery/

**Before start flashing.....**
- Backup all your internal storage
- GApps are included, so you don't need to flash them
- Download latest boot.img, vendor_boot.img, dtbo.img, supee_empty.img from here
- Links-: https://sourceforge.net/projects/infinity-x/files/tetris/16/recovery/

**Clean flash**
1. Reboot to fastboot
2. ```fastboot flash dtbo dtbo.img```
3. ```fastboot flash vendor_boot vendor_boot.img```
4. ```fastboot flash boot boot.img```
5. ```fastboot wipe-super super_empty.img```
6. Reboot to recovery
7. Select Factory reset and Format data/factory reset
8. Select Apply Update and Apply Update from ADB
9. Now connect your phone to PC and type ```adb sideload InfinityX.zip```
10. Reboot to system and enjoy :)

**Dirty flash** 
1. Reboot to recovery
2. Select Apply Update and Apply Update from ADB
3. Connect your phone to PC and flash rom with ```adb sideload infinityX.zip```
4. Reboot to system and enjoy :)

**OTA**
- Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)
