Device: Redmi Note 12 5G / Poco X5 5G (stone)

Flashing Instructions:

* Clean Flash:
- Use aosp recovery / orangefox by kami
- Wipe dalvik cache
- Flash rom
- Flash magisk ( optional)
- Do format data
- Reboot to system

* Dirty Flash:
- Reboot to recovery
- Wipe dalvik cache
- Flash rom
- Reboot to system
