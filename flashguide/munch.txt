Recommended Recovery: https://sourceforge.net/projects/recovery-for-xiaomi-devices/files/munch/twrp-3.7.1_12-v9.0_A15-munch-skkk.img
Recommended firmware: Included. Flashing any 1.0.5 Regional is okay.

Flashing Instructions:

* Clean Flash:
- Wipe Data
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (if you flashed vanilla and want google apps)
- Reboot

* Dirty Flash:
- Wipe Dalvik, Cache
- Flash ROM.zip
- Flash Gapps (if you flashed vanilla and want google apps)
- Reboot

* OTA:
1. Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)
