Device: OnePlus 12R / ACE 3 (aston)
Recommended Recovery: https://sourceforge.net/projects/infinity-x/files/aston/16/infinity_aston-recoveryimage.img

Flashing Instructions:

--- MUST BE ON OxygenOS 15 or ColorOS 15 before flashing this ---

* Clean Flash:
1. Reboot to recovery [AOSP Recovery Recommended] (Get from here: https://sourceforge.net/projects/infinity-x/files/aston/16/infinity_aston-recoveryimage.img)

2. Apply update -> adb sideload

3. adb sideload rom.zip

4. Format data

5. Reboot

* Dirty Flash:
1. Reboot to recovery

2. Apply update -> adb sideload

3. adb sideload rom.zip

4. Reboot

* OTA:
1. Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)
