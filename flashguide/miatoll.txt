#Flashing Instructions For Miatoll

~Clean Flash~

• Download & Flash Recovery
• Reboot to recovery
• Wipe cache, data, metadata, dalvik-cache
• Flash rom.zip
• Flash GAPPS if vanilla build
• Format Data
• Reboot System

~Dirty Flash~

• Reboot to recovery
• Wipe dalvik-cache
• Flash rom.zip
• Reboot system
• If using gapps or magisk before then reboot to recovery and flash zip.
