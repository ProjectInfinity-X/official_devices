----------FLASHING INSTRUCTIONS---------
• MUST be on Rui4 (F.06)

*ROM Flashing (clean)*
- Reboot to Bootloader
  >> fastboot --set-active=b
• Flash the dtbo,vendor_boot,boot images
- Go to bootloader
  » fastboot flash dtbo dtbo.img
  » fastboot flash boot boot.img 
  » fastboot flash vendor_boot vendor_boot.img
- Reboot to recovery
- Format data
- apply update> adb method
- adb sideload rom.zip  [adb sideload (drag and drop rom file)]
- Reboot to recovery
- Format data
- Reboot to System

*ROM Flashing (dirty)*
- Reboot to Bootloader 
  >> fastboot --set-active=b
- Reboot to Recovery
- apply update> adb method 
- adb sideload rom.zip  [adb sideload (drag and drop rom file)]
- Reboot to Recovery
- Reboot to System

*OTA*
- Simply upgrade via ota updater (either if pushed or via local update import)

Note:
• If "fastboot --set-active=b" fails , flash rom twice to flash in slot a (check the slot in recovery, before booting to system, MUST be a)
